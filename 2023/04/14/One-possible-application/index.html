I have a Volkswagen car, I start the engine, and it gives me 043 fault code. What does that mean?
The 043 fault code indicates that the engine is not running properly due to a problem with the fuel injection system. This could be caused by a faulty fuel injector, a clogged fuel filter, or a problem with the fuel pump. It is recommended to have the vehicle checked by a certified mechanic to diagnose and repair the issue.
What should I check after this code?
After the 043 fault code, you should check the fuel filter and fuel pump to ensure they are working properly. You should also check the fuel injectors to ensure they are not clogged or damaged. If the issue persists, you should take your vehicle to a certified mechanic for further diagnosis and repair.
